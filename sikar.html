Welcome to Sikar

Sikar is a heritage city in the Shekhawati region of Rajasthan, known for its painted havelis, ancient temples, and vibrant Rajasthani culture. Once ruled by the Shekhawat Rajputs, Sikar combines traditional architecture with modern education centers. It's often called the "Education City" of Rajasthan due to its many coaching institutes and schools.
