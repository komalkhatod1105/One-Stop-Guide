Welcome to Kota

Discover Kota, the coaching capital of India, known for its educational excellence and the scenic beauty of the Chambal River. Experience a perfect blend of academics, culture, and natural beauty.

About Kota

Kota is renowned as India's coaching hub, attracting students from across the country. Beyond academics, the city offers beautiful riverside walks along the Chambal River, historic forts, and vibrant local markets.


Key Attractions:
  • Chambal River boat rides
  • 7 Wonders Park
  • City Palace
  • Rajiv Gandhi Crocodile Park
  • Local markets and cuisine
