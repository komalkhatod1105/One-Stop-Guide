Welcome to Pune

Pune, often referred to as the "Oxford of the East," is a vibrant city in Maharashtra, India. It is known for its rich history, educational institutions, and cultural diversity. The city has a unique blend of modernity and tradition, making it a popular destination for students and professionals alike. Pune is known for its educational institutions, rich Maratha history, and vibrant cultural heritage.

Pune is famous for its prestigious educational institutions, rich Maratha history, and vibrant cultural heritage. The city is home to several historical landmarks, including the Aga Khan Palace and Shaniwar Wada, which reflect its glorious past.


Things to do:

  • Visit the historic Shaniwar Wada
  • Explore the Aga Khan Palace
  • Stroll through the beautiful Osho Ashram
  • Enjoy the scenic views at Sinhagad Fort
  • Experience the vibrant nightlife in Koregaon Park
Pune's cultural scene is vibrant, with numerous festivals celebrated throughout the year, showcasing its rich traditions. The city also offers a variety of culinary delights, from traditional Maharashtrian dishes to modern cafes and restaurants.
